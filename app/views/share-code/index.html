Share your identity information

Use this service to prove to someone your:

  • name
  • date of birth
  • address
  • nationality

Create an identity 'check code' to share your details with someone.

A 'check code' gives someone permission to check your details from GOV.UK.

For example when opening a bank account, paying council tax or getting a phone contract.

The 'check code' will be valid for 21 days.

You'll need either:

  • passport
  • driving licence
  • online bank login details
  • home country ID. For example Dutch DigiD

Before you start

You can also register by post.

The online service is also available in Welsh (Cymraeg).

You can’t register for this service if you’re in the UK illegally.
